# Executive Summary – SAP Cutover & Hypercare

## Context

SAP S/4HANA programs involve high operational risk during go-live, requiring strong coordination across technical and business teams.

## Key Challenges

- Lack of alignment between planning and execution  
- Limited visibility of critical risks  
- Reactive incident management  

## Approach

This repository provides a structured, execution-driven framework covering:

- Cutover planning and governance  
- War room operating model  
- Hypercare stabilization framework  

## Business Impact

- Reduced go-live risk  
- Faster issue resolution  
- Improved stakeholder alignment  
- Enhanced decision-making  

## Positioning

Designed for complex SAP environments with multiple systems and global stakeholders.
