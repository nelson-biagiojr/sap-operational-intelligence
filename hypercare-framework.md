# SAP Hypercare Framework (Enterprise)

## Incident Management Flow

![Hypercare Incident Flow](assets/hypercare-incident-management-flow.png)

## Objective

Stabilize system operations post go-live and ensure business continuity.

## Duration

Typically 2–4 weeks (depending on complexity)

## Governance

- Dedicated Hypercare Manager
- Daily Executive Reporting
- SLA-driven support model

## Incident Management

### Classification

- Critical (system down / financial impact)
- High (process blocked)
- Medium (partial degradation)
- Low (minor issues)

### Process

1. Incident logging (ServiceNow / Azure DevOps)
2. Triage
3. Assignment
4. Resolution
5. Validation

## Reporting

### Daily Report Includes

- Total incidents
- Critical incidents
- Resolved vs Open
- Key risks
- Business impact

## Exit Criteria

- Incident volume stabilized
- No critical issues
- SLA consistently met
- Business sign-off

## Transition to AMS

- Knowledge transfer completed
- Documentation delivered
- Support ownership transferred
