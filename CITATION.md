## How to Cite This Framework

If you use, reference, or build upon the SAP Operational Intelligence framework
in academic work, professional publications, internal documentation,
or public content, please use one of the formats below.

---

## Preferred Citation Formats

**APA (7th edition)**

cutover governance, stabilization telemetry, and executive visibility in
SAP S/4HANA transformation programs*. GitHub.
https://github.com/nelson-biagiojr/sap-cutover-framework

## Citing Specific Documents

When referencing a specific section or document within the framework,
use *SAP Operational Intelligence* as the parent work and identify
the specific document as a chapter or section within it.

**APA**

Biagio Junior, N. (2026). Telemetry model. In *SAP Operational Intelligence*.
GitHub. https://nelson-biagiojr.github.io/sap-operational-intelligence/operational-intelligence/telemetry-model.html

---

BIAGIO JUNIOR, Nelson. Telemetry Model. In: ______. **SAP Operational
Intelligence**. São Paulo, 2026. Disponível em:
